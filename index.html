Hello! This is team Kyoto from Japan! Our project title is Cut Flower Loss.
Our project’s goal is to propose comprehensive solution to the losses that cut flower is facing.
Cut flower appears in many places of our daily life, but on rearing it or keeping it fresh, people including flower shoppers have some difficulties due to virus infection or aging of flower itself. In order to efficiently tackle the problems, we plan to devise easy detection system utilizing CRISPR-Cas and isothermal PCR. Futhermore, with the aid of RNAi, we also aim to wipe out the pest damage to flowers by targeting essential genes of bugs. In addition, innovative inhibition system against plants’ aging mechanism represented by ethylene aging system, that will keep cut flower fresh even long after being cut.
Last but not least, we are looking for iGEM teams that will collaborate with us throughout this project, so please feel free to contact us anytime!
